# Living Works by the Word

A generative architecture and living systems design project combining morphogenetic simulation, philosophy, scripture, and business strategy — built around the idea that organisms, not machines, are the proper model for design.

## Business Plan

Targeting the intersection of synthetic biology, AI drug design, and bioinformatics — positioning "Morphological BioCAD" as a new category nobody else occupies.

- Market: $20B+ Synthetic Biology, $10B+ AI Drug Design, $15B+ Bioinformatics
- Revenue tiers: Academic $50-200/mo, Startup $500/mo, Enterprise $2000+/mo + IP licensing
- Key differentiator: organism-scale growth simulation and design (not molecule or gene level)
- Source documents in `business plan pitch prompts/`
