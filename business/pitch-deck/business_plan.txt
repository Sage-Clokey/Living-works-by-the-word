An AI-driven platform for designing biological systems, organisms, and living materials.

* Autodesk for biology
* Blender for living structures

# Core Technology

## 1 — Morphogenesis Engine

Software that simulates biological growth in 3D.

Capabilities:

* branching systems (trees, coral, vasculature)
* cellular growth
* nutrient gradients
* biomechanical forces
* developmental pathways

Outputs:

* 3D models
* genetic pathway suggestions
* growth predictions

---

## 2 — Natural Language Bio Design

Users describe biological structures.

Example:

> “Grow a self-supporting dome structure using fungal mycelium optimized for humidity resistance.”

The system converts that into:

* growth models
* gene pathway suggestions
* structural simulations

---

## 3 — BioCAD Platform

Computer-aided design for living systems.

Features:

* organism blueprinting
* gene network visualization
* tissue architecture modeling
* environment simulation

---

## 4 — Bioinformatics Integration

Connect existing tools:

* genome browsers
* pathway analysis
* single-cell data
* metabolic modeling

Transform them into **design tools instead of analysis tools**.
