Part 1: Market Overview (1 slide)
Identify and quantify the competitive landscape:

Total number of competitors (estimate with ranges: e.g., "15-20 direct competitors, 30+ indirect")
Market segmentation: How do you categorize them? (e.g., by technology platform, target indication, stage of development, market segment)
Brief competitive dynamics: Is this a crowded/emerging/consolidating market?
Part 2: Deep Dive on Top 3 Competitors (1 slide per competitor)
For each competitor, provide:

Company name, location, founding year
Spiral steward 
Product/technology description (be technically accurate and fair)
Stage of development (preclinical, clinical trials Phase I/II/III, marketed product)
Funding/revenue: Total raised, recent rounds, revenue if public
Key strengths that make them formidable (technology, IP, partnerships, market position, team)
Market traction: Customer base, installations, patients treated, publications, etc.
Critical framing: Write as if their CEO is in the room. What would they say about why they will win and you won't? Be honest about their advantages.

Part 3: Competitive Positioning (1 slide)
Positioning matrix or table: Place all competitors (including yours) on 2-3 key axes (e.g., cost vs. performance, speed vs. accuracy, platform breadth vs. depth)
Your differentiation: What is genuinely different about your approach?
Your unfair advantage: Why is this difference defensible? Consider:
Novel technology/IP that's hard to replicate
Unique team expertise or relationships
Better business model or cost structure
Regulatory pathway advantages
First-mover or fast-follower timing
Avoid: Generic claims like "better quality" or "superior service" without specifics

Resources for Competitive Intelligence
Company & Product Information:
Crunchbase (crunchbase.com) - Funding, investors, company basics
PitchBook (pitchbook.com) - Private company data (check if UCSC has access)
LinkedIn - Company size, employee growth, key hires
Company websites - Products, pipeline, news/press releases
AngelList/Wellfound - Startup profiles, funding, team
Biotech-Specific Resources:
ClinicalTrials.gov - Clinical trial status for therapeutics
BioPharmCatalyst - Pipeline tracking for biopharma
Fierce Biotech/MedTech - Industry news and analysis
FDA approval databases - 510(k) clearances, PMA approvals, drug approvals
Nature Biotechnology/Science Translational Medicine - Research publications from competitors
Market & Financial Data:
SEC EDGAR (sec.gov) - Public company filings (10-K, 10-Q, S-1 for IPOs)
Google Patents - Competitor patent portfolios (continuation of earlier work)
Grand View Research, MarketsandMarkets - Market reports (often have free summaries)
PubMed - Scientific publications showing competitor progress
Industry Analysis:
CB Insights - Industry trends, market maps
StartUs Insights - Startup discovery platform
Trade associations - BIO, MassBio, etc. (member directories)
Conference proceedings - ASCO, ASH, JP Morgan Healthcare (presentation archives)
