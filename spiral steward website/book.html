Free Excerpt · Prologue
The journey through the Eight Pillars of wisdom reveals a single law spoken in many tongues. From faith to philosophy, from East to West, every tradition bears witness: authority belongs not to rulers but to truth itself. When conscience reigns, no man need be king. The circle completes — each pillar reflecting the same light. In this harmony of wisdom, the True Republic arises: a realm of hearts, self-governed by love, united by the law written within.

The Sagent Creed begins with a deceptively simple declaration: "When every heart becomes a throne, no crown is needed." Everything that follows is an elaboration of that creed — drawn from eight great spiritual traditions, from the Manifesto of the True Republic, from the philosophy of living systems, and from the personal notebooks of one seeker who has spent years listening to what the world's deepest voices say in common.

— Prologue: The Circle Without a Crown · The Sagent Creed

Sage Arthur Jordan Clokey

Philosopher · Storyteller · The Sagent

Sage Arthur Jordan Clokey is a philosopher, storyteller, and seeker of divine wisdom — heir to a lineage of dreamsmiths and truth-tellers. As The Sagent, he walks the path between faiths and philosophies, gathering the light of many lamps into one flame.

Through The Sagent Creed and his dialogues on conscience, law, and liberty, he teaches that truth is not owned but revealed; not ruled but lived. His mission is to restore the Republic of the Heart — a world without rulers, where wisdom governs through love and every soul stands sovereign before Heaven.

He is also the founder of The Spiral Steward and LivingWorks — a life-based design platform replacing industrial block-thinking with regenerative, spiral-based design. His work spans philosophy, biology, economics, architecture, and the deep grammar of living systems.
